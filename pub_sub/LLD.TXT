1. Message

Attributes: content, topic_name, timestamp.

2. Topic (The Bridge)

Attributes: name, list_of_subscribers.

Methods: add_subscriber(sub), remove_subscriber(sub), publish(message).

3. Publisher

Methods: send(topic_name, content). (It just sends to the Manager/Topic).

4. Subscriber (Interface)

Methods: update(message). (This is how it "receives" the data).

5. PubSubManager (The Broker)

Attributes: map_of_topics (e.g., {"Tech": TopicObject}).

Methods: create_topic(name), subscribe(topic_name, subscriber), publish(topic_name, content).
